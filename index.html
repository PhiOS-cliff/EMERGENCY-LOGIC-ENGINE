What ELE Is and Is Not
ELE IS

A procedural overlay on top of AI

• Uses Emergency Management logic
• Organizes messy conditions
• Finds the next procedural minimum
• Helps you stay in control

ELE IS NOT

An AI chatbot or replacement

• Not a general chatbot
• Does not give legal/medical advice
• Does not decide for you
• Does not chase novelty or opinions

How to Use

Simple 4-Step Process

  1. Download the Bootloader V0.1 and Controls from the Library above.
  2. Copy the Bootloader prompt into any AI chat (ChatGPT, Grok, Claude, etc.).
  3. Activate by declaring your condition.
  4. Cycle between SOURCE → ELI → ELE as needed.
Example Bootloader Activation 
I am [Your Role, e.g. Emergency Manager / Lawyer / Property Owner].
The condition is: [Describe the situation clearly].

ENTER ELE

After pasting the full Bootloader prompt (from the PDF), just declare your role + condition and type ENTER ELE.
