The Problem

AI drifts because it tries to think in every direction at once.

General AI is powerful, but complex work often requires different kinds of thinking at different moments. Discovery, collaboration, orientation, imagination, and escalation are not the same operation.

Normal AI blends the work.

It explains, brainstorms, diagnoses, predicts, summarizes, and recommends in the same motion. That can be useful in simple conversations, but unstable in high-load environments.

Version 11.2 separates the work.

The operator chooses the cognitive state. The system stops trying to be everything at once and performs the kind of thinking needed now.

The System

A manual transmission for cognition.

The Pilot remains in command. The system does not replace expertise. It stabilizes the relationship between human judgment and machine intelligence.
