Hold Short

Advancement requires clearance.

The Emergency Logic Engine preserves system integrity through hold-short behavior. Incomplete inputs, unresolved dependencies, contradictory records, and missing authority produce a hold-short status.

Hold Short means the Engine has identified a required condition still awaiting verification. Advancement resumes through resolution of that condition.

The same logic that supports clearance also supports restraint. Procedural minimums govern advancement. Missing procedural minimums govern hold-short status.
