Stephens v. Texas

Public Record on Emergency Management, Courthouse Security, and Constitutional Continuity

Core Question

When a documented emergency is presented to county government, must the system receive it, acknowledge it, assess it, and identify responsibility?

This case began with a structural hazard at 122 West Houston Street in Linden and grew into questions about emergency intake under Chapter 418 and courthouse operations.
